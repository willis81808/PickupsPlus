# PickupsPlus
Enhance your matches with pickups!

## Pickups

- Speed
	- Increases speed by 50% for 5 seconds
- Damage
	- Doubles damage for 5 seconds
- Ammo
	- Adds 5 to your max ammo for 5 seconds
- Health
	- Heals all damage
- Shield
	- Recharges shield cooldown
